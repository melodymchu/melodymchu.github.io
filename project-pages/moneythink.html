CMU Moneythink Rebrand

September 2024

As the Director of Design, I led a comprehensive rebrand to elevate the club’s visual identity and enhance its alignment with organizational goals. This project included designing a new logo, selecting modern fonts, and establishing a cohesive brand identity to improve recognition and appeal. I created a set of versatile templates and eye-catching posters, as well as engaging social media content to drive event promotion and outreach. To bring the brand vision to life, I recruited and guided a team of student designers, overseeing creative direction and ensuring consistency across all digital and print communications. This rebranding initiative successfully boosted the club’s visibility, creating a professional and memorable presence that resonated with members and the broader community.
