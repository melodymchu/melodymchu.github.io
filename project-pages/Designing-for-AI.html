Designing for AI Products and Services

October - December 2023

Course Overview Artificial Intelligence Design Course, Carnegie Mellon University In this course, we explored the emerging role of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in enhancing user experiences and creating innovative products. AI, often in the form of machine learning, is becoming increasingly significant in shaping people’s interactions with services, curating news feeds, automating routine tasks, and augmenting decision-making processes. However, working with AI as a design material presents unique challenges. Unlike traditional design materials like wood, paper, or digital interfaces, AI lacks physicality and direct malleability, making it challenging for designers to understand and engage with intuitively. The course focused on making students more effective at conversing with AI—understanding its capabilities, recognizing opportunities for AI enhancement, and giving form to new AI-powered products and services. Through a combination of hands-on projects, experimentation with different AI technologies, and design thinking, we learned to integrate AI in a meaningful way to solve real-world problems.
